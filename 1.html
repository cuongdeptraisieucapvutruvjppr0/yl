Club History

Liverpool FC was founded in 1892 and quickly became a powerhouse of English football.

The club has won 6 Champions League titles and 19 Premier League titles.

Key Players

Liverpool has world-class players such as Salah, Van Dijk, and Alisson — key figures in recent success.

Anfield Stadium

Anfield is known for its electric atmosphere and The Kop stand.

Fans sing “You'll Never Walk Alone” before every home match.
